CCC.io Trading Signals

Trading signals are trading ideas for a certain pair produced by our team which are broken down into suggested compounded entries and targets to either buy or sell based on Trading Analysis.

Our suggested trades are always backed up by Trading Analysis like S/R, patterns and technical indicators. Whenever possible, given our 24/7 monitoring of news, they will also be supported by Fundamental Analysis.

We also indicate the trading time, since some setups may be short term (< 1 week), mid term (1-4 weeks) or long term (1-4 months).

Example

  • MATIC/BTC 100x (10,000%) trade example. Simple and to the point.
  • Entry 50-70 sats
  • Target 570 zone
  • Time frame 4-8 months

Stop-Loss wasn’t needed in this case because of strong support level and dip entry, along with the market sentiment that was very bullish towards the 2021 Bull cycle - after 2020 BTC halving event.
